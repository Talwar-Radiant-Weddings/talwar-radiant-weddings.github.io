ABOUT ME

More about your wedding planner, Ali Talwar:

Hello! I'm Ali and I'm so happy you found Talwar Radiant Weddings! I decided to pursue wedding planning full time after my career in International Development came to a screeching halt. Thankfully, I have been lucky to gain experience in the industry by assisting several seasoned wedding planners in the area and by planning weddings for my friends. I have found such a passion in this career and find complete joy in working with couples to create their unique weddings!

Favorite part of a wedding: The first look! Whether it is before the ceremony or on the aisle, it is so special to see how the couple look at each other as this moment they have been waiting for finally starts happening.

Favorite hobbies: Binging a great TV show with my husband; Going on long walks with friends in Rock Creek Park; Reading fantasy boooks; Biking, swimming, hiking, or even just sitting in the park on a nice day.

Fun fact about me: I have lived in 13 different cities because my parents were in the Navy. I even moved to Thailand for two years while serving in the Peace Corps as an English Teacher!
